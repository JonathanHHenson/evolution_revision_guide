# Lesson 3 — Natural selection, drift and speciation

[Watch the complete lesson](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K2JCO6eXans) (4:17:13).

This lesson follows new variation into populations. Erika distinguishes selection from chance, uses measured field populations to connect fitness with allele-frequency change, and then explains why species boundaries and the route to speciation are expected to be gradual and sometimes untidy.

1. [Will's opening presentation](00-wills-opening.md) — his research after the mutation lesson and Erika's corrections.

4. [Divergence and speciation](03-divergence-and-speciation.md) — species concepts, reproductive barriers, language analogy and branching lineages.
